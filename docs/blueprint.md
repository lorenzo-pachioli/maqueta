# **App Name**: Transpo Marketplace

## Core Features:

- Role Selection: Allow users to select their role (customer or worker) to tailor the experience.
- Customer Onboarding (Mock): A simple form to collect customer information like name, location, and preferred transportation type.
- Worker Onboarding (Mock): A form for workers to input their name, service type, hourly rate, availability, and work zone.
- Service Worker List: List available workers in cards with name, service type, rating, price, and distance.
- Filtering and Sorting: Enable filtering by service type and sorting by rating, distance, or price.
- Worker Dashboard: Provide a dashboard for workers to view their rate, availability, services, and status.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft yellow (#FADA5E) to bring warmth and approachability, avoiding the corporate feel.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) for a clean, minimalist aesthetic.
- Accent color: Pale orange (#FFC872) for interactive elements and highlights, ensuring they stand out without overwhelming the user.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for clear readability, especially on mobile.
- Mobile-first approach with a focus on large, touchable components and a bottom navigation bar.
- Use of cards and grids to present information in a simple, digestible manner.
- Subtle transitions and animations for a smooth, app-like user experience.
